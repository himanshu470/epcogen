ABOUT EPCOGEN

EPCOGEN Pvt Ltd is an Engineering Service Company, performing Pre-Bid Engineering, FEED & Detailed Engineering, primarily for the Oil & Gas, Refineries, Petrochemicals and Power Projects.

Our team of experts in the areas of engineering, management and construction bring you the insight and advice you need for your project.

  • Established by an experienced group of industry-experts

  • Multi-discipline Engineers and Designers with vast and versatile Industry experience.

  • Oil & Gas (Onshore and Offshore), Refinery, Petrochemicals, Power plant, Process plants, other Industrial Infrastructure etc.

  • Offices in Chennai, Hyderabad & Bengaluru.

  • Fabrication units at Chennai, Hyderabad.

  • Collaboration with Educational Institutions.

ADITYA KESANUPALLI

CFO

ADITYA KESANUPALLI

    Professional Background
  • Aditya is a versatile professional with a diverse background and a strong track record of accomplishments across various industries. He completed his Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from SRM Engineering Chennai in 2011.
  • His career journey took him through various roles and experiences, shaping his expertise and knowledge. He started as an Instrumentation Engineer for Petrofac International in Sharjah and Abu Dhabi, gaining practical insights into the field and contributing to projects in locations as diverse as Bulgaria and Turkmenistan.
  • Recognizing the value of a holistic education, Aditya pursued a post-graduation in Liberal Arts from Ashoka University, Delhi in 2015. This educational choice highlights his commitment to well-rounded learning and his openness to diverse fields of study.
  • Aditya's professional journey also includes impactful roles in the public and private sectors. He served as a consultant for the Planning department of the Government of Andhra Pradesh, working closely with the Chief Secretary in 2016, demonstrating his dedication to public service and governance.
  • His foray into the private sector saw him taking on various leadership roles. Aditya served as a Business Development Manager in India for Go-Trust, a Taiwanese data encryption firm, for 3 years. This role showcased his ability to drive business growth and develop strategic partnerships.
  • Aditya's passion for societal impact led him to work as the Marketing and Communications Director for Vision India Foundation, a non-profit organization, for 2 years. This role emphasized his commitment to social change and effective communication.
  • He further expanded his expertise by serving as a Business Development Manager for Digital Trust, a US-based digital identity firm, for 2 years, contributing to the realm of digital security and identity management.
  • In 2018, Aditya took a bold step by establishing Safyrus Media, a marketing agency based in Hyderabad. This entrepreneurial endeavor showcased his leadership skills and his understanding of the marketing landscape.
  • Continuing his entrepreneurial journey, he founded Epcogen Private Limited in 2019, a venture with a presence in Chennai and Hyderabad. As the Director and Chief Information and Finance Officer, Aditya is driving the company's strategic direction and financial growth.
  • With a dynamic background encompassing engineering, liberal arts, public service, and diverse leadership roles, Aditya embodies adaptability, innovation, and a commitment to making a positive impact in various domains.
